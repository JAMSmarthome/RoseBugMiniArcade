Rose-Bug Mini Arcade — Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

1. Introduction

Rose-Bug Mini Arcade is a collection of offline mini-games designed for kids. We care about privacy — especially children’s privacy — and we do not collect personal data.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • ❌ No personal information
  • ❌ No location data
  • ❌ No analytics or tracking
  • ❌ No advertising identifiers

3. Local Data Stored

The only data stored is on the device: game progress, high scores, mute setting and last selected game. This data never leaves the device.

4. Children’s Privacy

The app is suitable for children and complies with COPPA/GDPR-K principles by avoiding any data collection or external tracking.

5. Contact

If you have questions or privacy concerns, please email jvzdevelopment@gmail.com.
